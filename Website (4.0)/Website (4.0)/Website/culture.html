Our Personal Code of Excellence

  1. I am a Suryojasvi – Devoted to Brilliance in my Profession.
  2. I am Grateful & feel Blessed for my Customers, my Team & my Responsibilities.
  3. Everyday I am focused & Inspired:
    • To Learn & Spread Skills & Knowledge
    • To Solve Ever Larger Challenges
    • To create greater happiness, success & abundance
    • To be more and more useful to my team, clients and the World
  4. Wisdom & Grace shines through me in my thought, word, and deed.
  5. I keep my word and my promise.
  6. I am a Suryojasvi, We are Suryojasvis.
