Our History

A Decade of Evolution

From humble beginnings to a diversified conglomerate, our path has been defined by vision, resilience, and growth.

2011

Inception

Wyzmindz commences operations, focusing on Managed Services in Work Force Management (WFM), Quality, Training, and Consulting.

2012

Future Focus

Strategic shift towards investing in future technologies, laying the groundwork for innovation.

2015-16

Optimization

Invested in and developed the AutoSherpa and TransMon portfolios. Successfully exited the Call Center & WFM managed services business to focus on products.

2017

Product Launch

Full suite of products developed and launched for AutoSherpa and TransMon Lines of Business.

2020

Market Leadership

Recognized as India's most preferred Qualification Monitoring Product.

2022

Dominance

Achieved status as India's most preferred Auto CRM in the market.

2024

Expansion

Started building next-gen products (FinGaruda, Vidur, Alertbee). Launched new startups: RAAAI and Yocoya.

...and the journey continues.

Suryojasvi Group is a diversified conglomerate committed to excellence across various sectors since 2011.
